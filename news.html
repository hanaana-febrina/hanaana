The Influence of Dangdut on Stress Levels Among Oxford Students

A recent study at Oxford University has revealed intriguing findings on the impact of dangdut music on student stress levels. Researchers conducted surveys and focus groups, examining how the vibrant Indonesian music genre affects the emotional well-being of students. The results showed that listening to dangdut significantly reduced stress among participants. Many students reported feeling more relaxed and uplifted after engaging with the music, often using it as a coping mechanism during exam periods. Experts believe that the lively rhythms and catchy melodies of dangdut create a positive atmosphere, encouraging social interaction and emotional expression. As students seek ways to manage academic pressures, incorporating music like dangdut into their routines may offer a valuable tool for improving mental health.
