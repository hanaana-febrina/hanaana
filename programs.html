Live Dangdut Event at Oxford Campus Cimahi

Event Title: Dangdut Night Extravaganza

Date: Saturday, November 15, 2024
Time: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Venue: Oxford Campus Auditorium, Cimahi Description:
Join us for an exciting evening of live dangdut music at Oxford Campus Cimahi! Experience the vibrant rhythms and energetic performances from top dangdut artists. This event aims to bring students together, promote cultural exchange, and provide a fun way to unwind from academic stress.
Schedule:

  • 6:00 PM: Doors Open
  • 6:30 PM: Opening Performance
  • 7:30 PM: Guest Artist Show
  • 8:30 PM: Audience Participation and Dance Contest
  • 9:30 PM: Closing Performance
Tickets:
- Students: $5
- General Admission: $10
(Tickets available at the campus student center or online)
Don’t miss this chance to enjoy great music, dance, and connect with fellow students!
